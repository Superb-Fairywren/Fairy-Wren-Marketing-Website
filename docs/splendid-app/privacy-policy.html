Privacy Policy

Splendid App

Last Updated: November 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Splendid App ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for our mobile application. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information or user data.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information or user data. Splendid App is designed to function entirely on your device without transmitting any personal information to our servers or third parties.

2.1 No Personal Information Collection

We do not require you to create accounts, provide personal details, or share any identifying information to use our app.

2.2 No Automatic Data Collection

We do not collect device information, usage analytics, crash reports, or any other automatically generated data. All app functionality operates locally on your device.

2.3 No Third-Party Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, there is no information to share with third parties, advertisers, or analytics services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We do not use your information because we do not collect any. Our app operates entirely on your device without sending any data to external servers. All features and functionality work locally without requiring data transmission.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your information because we do not collect any. Since no personal data is collected, stored, or processed by our app, there is no information to share with third parties, service providers, or any other entities.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, there are no data security concerns related to our servers or databases. All app functionality operates locally on your device, and any data you create within the app remains on your device under your control.

6. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal information because we do not collect any. Any data you create while using the app is stored locally on your device and is not accessible to us.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Since we do not collect any personal information, traditional privacy rights such as access, correction, deletion, or data portability do not apply. You maintain complete control over any data created within the app, as it remains on your device.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information.

9. Third-Party Links and Services

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not operated by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you access.

10. International Data Transfers

No international data transfers occur because we do not collect or transmit any personal information.

11. California Privacy Rights

California residents have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). However, since we do not collect any personal information, these rights do not apply to our app. We do not sell, share, or process any personal information.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
